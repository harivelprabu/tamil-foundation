Welcome! 🙏 வணக்கம்

This programme teaches Tamil from the very beginning — no prior knowledge needed. Work through one training programme each week. Each week has short lessons broken into exam-style sections, then a test at the end. After the test you'll see your score, and every wrong answer is explained. Each completed test is saved automatically for the teacher to review any time from Teacher Access below.

How the tests work

  • All questions are multiple-choice — just tap the correct answer (no Tamil typing needed).
  • Each test is split into Sections A, B, C… like a real exam.
  • After submitting the test, correct answers show in green and mistakes in red, each with an explanation.
  • The completed test is then saved for the teacher, viewable under Teacher Access.
